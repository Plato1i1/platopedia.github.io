(15/03/23) A new bot emerges - Peacemaker

Make way for the new sheriff in town. Peacemaker is locked and loaded and ready to serve a piping hot plate of justice to anything that stands in its way. Peacemaker is a medium weight bot with high health points and high accuracy. It uses it's whip to immobilize enemies and bullets to do multiple damage.
